About

Bridging scales —
from turbulent flows to colliding protons

I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Mechanical & Aeronautical Engineering at the University of Pretoria, where I lead research programmes spanning computational mechanics, artificial intelligence, climate science, high-energy physics, and quantum technologies.

My work is characterised by a commitment to high-performance computing as an enabler — whether resolving multiphase flow in a hydrocyclone, reconstructing particle collision events at the ATLAS detector at CERN, forecasting urban heat islands with foundation AI models, or developing quantum sensing and computing programmes with real-world impact.

Previously I was a Staff Research Scientist at IBM Research Africa, where I co-developed the open-source Granite Geospatial Land Surface Temperature model, a fine-tuned Earth Observation Foundation Model now publicly available on HuggingFace. I hold a BEng (Mechanical Engineering), BSc Honours (Applied Mathematics), MEng, and DIng (Computational Fluid & Granular Dynamics), all from the University of Johannesburg.

I am Vice-President of the Executive Committee of the South African Association for Theoretical and Applied Mechanics (SAAM) and Vice-President of the South African National IUTAM (International Union of Theoretical and Applied Mechanics) Committee. I am an author on more than 200 publications as a member of the ATLAS Collaboration at CERN.

Research

Areas of investigation

A portfolio spanning four decades of scale — from quantum phenomena to planetary climate systems.

🌀

Computational Fluid Dynamics

Multiphase and granular flow modelling in process equipment including hydrocyclones, fluidised beds, and biomedical devices. Development of Lattice Boltzmann Method (LBM) and Euler–Euler frameworks on HPC infrastructure.

LBM Multiphase Flow HPC OpenFOAM
🌍

AI & Climate Intelligence

Foundation model development for geospatial Earth observation, including the open-source Granite Geospatial Land Surface Temperature model. Urban heat island detection and characterisation using vision transformers and auxiliary feature injection.

Foundation Models Prithvi / ViT Urban Heat Islands EOFM
⚛️

High-Energy Physics

Contributing member of the ATLAS Collaboration at CERN. Research spans detector environmental monitoring for the ITk upgrade, Higgs boson characterisation, top quark physics, and the landmark observation of quantum entanglement with top quarks (Nature, 2024).

ATLAS / CERN Higgs Boson Top Quarks HL-LHC
🔬

Quantum Computing & Sensing

New research programmes at the University of Pretoria in quantum computing and quantum sensing, with applications in engineering and the physical sciences. Building on connections between ML pipelines developed for particle physics and emerging quantum architectures.

Quantum Computing Quantum Sensing Quantum Information
🏥

Biomedical & Health CFD

CFD modelling of respiratory droplet transmission and UV germicidal irradiation for infection control, ventriculoperitoneal shunt fluid dynamics, and lung acoustics diagnosis. Collaboration with the Perinatal HIV Research Unit (PHRU), Wits.

Droplet Dynamics UVGI COVID-19 Biomedical Flow
📐

Engineering Education

Scholarship of teaching and learning with a focus on design literacy, academic voice, online and blended learning, and decolonisation of engineering curricula in post-Apartheid South Africa.

Design Literacy Blended Learning Decolonisation SASEE
